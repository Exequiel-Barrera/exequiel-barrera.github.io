What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is the ability to understand, use, and manage your emotions in positive ways. It helps you communicate effectively, empathize with others, overcome challenges, and defuse conflict.

EQ vs IQ

IQ measures logical reasoning, problem-solving, and analytical skills. EQ focuses on emotional awareness, empathy, and social interactions. Both are important, but EQ often determines how well we apply our IQ in real-world situations.

Why is EQ Important?

EQ is essential for building strong relationships, achieving personal and professional goals, and leading effectively. People with high EQ tend to manage stress better, resolve conflicts smoothly, and inspire others more easily.
