Welcome to Easy Trendy 3D Prints!

As a I just started in this new hobby, i believe all of us newbies we look for the easiest and less complicated 3D prints models at the beginning of the journey When i say easy, i mean that basically don't need much tweaking on settings and are easy to print without much hassle. I will be sharing part of my journey and some large models that i have found that are easy to print and look great. As well as recommended websites to freely download amazing 3D models.

Where to find free 3D model to be printed

  • Thingiverse - A popular repository for 3D models; free and paid models
  • MyMiniFactory - A curated platform with high-quality models.
  • Cults3D - A marketplace for 3D models, including free and paid options.
