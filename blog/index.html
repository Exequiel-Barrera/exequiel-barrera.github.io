Some useful tips before we start printing our models

  • Make sure your printer is calibrated correctly.(some printer have a pre calibration mode on before start printing)
  • Use the proper filament regarding the settings you have set on your printer as default.
  • Check that nozzle is clean.
  • Start with smaller models to get familiar with your printer.
  • Clean bed plate and make sure is free of previous residues
  • Check if you 3D model needs support if this has some over hanging parts
  • Add some glue on your bed plate if you just want to prevent possibly printing model coming loose during the process

Where to find free 3D model to be printed

  • Thingiverse - A popular repository for 3D models; free and paid models
  • MyMiniFactory - A curated platform with high-quality models.
  • Cults3D - A marketplace for 3D models, including free and paid options.
